About Catherina

To provide client-focused fitness training with excellent quality. Create individualized programs that combine the latest cutting-edge methods in the industry to ensure optimal results and help any client achieve their goal.

With more than 20 years of experience in the fitness industry, Catherina Vaucheret belongs to the new generation of personal trainers inspired by emerging techniques that integrate core, flexibility, balance, cardio, and strength in compact and efficient sessions adapted to all. Certified with the acclaimed NASM (National Academy of Sports Medicine), NCSF (the National Council on Strength and Fitness), as well as her credentials as a Sport Nutrition Specialist, Catherina and her Fitness Factor target all the body parts and fundamentals of biomechanics.

As a Cable Flexibility specialist, Catherina takes training to a completely new level and will design specific programs to help you reach your goals and get results beyond expectations with creative and fun sessions. Essential sport nutrition advice will spice up your training.

Languages: Fluent in English, Spanish, and French
